Brief CV

Brief C.V.

Selected appointments, education, honors, and service.

Education

  • 1987 Ph.D. in Physics and Mathematics (Biomathematics), Moscow State University, USSR
  • 1982 B.S. in Physics (Biophysics), Moscow State University, USSR

Positions

  • 2020–present: External Faculty, Complexity Science Hub Vienna
  • 2018–present: Director, Center for the Dynamics of Social Complexity, University of Tennessee
  • 2015–present: Research Associate, School of Anthropology, University of Oxford
  • 2007–present: Distinguished Professor, University of Tennessee
  • 2008–2021: Associate Director for Scientific Activities, NIMBioS

Awards and recognition

  • President’s Award, American Society of Naturalists (1999)
  • Research and Creative Achievement Award, UTK (2001)
  • Research and Creative Achievement Award, UTK College of Arts and Sciences (2005)
  • Guggenheim Fellowship (2008)
  • AGA Key Distinguished Lecture, American Genetic Association (2013)
  • Fellow, American Academy of Arts and Sciences (2017)

Editorial roles

  • Associate Editor: American Naturalist, Evolution, BMC Evolutionary Biology, Cliodynamics, Human Behavior and Evolution, PNAS Nexus

Professional service

Service includes manuscript review for a wide range of journals, grant review for NSF, ERC, NERC, ARC, FWF, DFG, and other agencies, and organizing workshops and working groups in evolutionary biology, social complexity, sustainability, cultural evolution, and social norms.
